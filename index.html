Our Story

About Ravenger

Ravenger eSports was founded in 2011, built by players who treated competition as a craft rather than a hobby. For over a decade, the organization has stood for one idea: results are earned, never given.

In 2026, Ravenger made the move into competitive eSports, fielding a Counter-Strike 2 roster assembled to compete at the highest level the org has ever pursued. The team made its debut in ESEA Season 58, marking the start of a new chapter built on the same standard the organization was founded on.

Every scrim, every scouting session, every round — the mission stays the same. Earn every win.
